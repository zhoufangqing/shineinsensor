About Us
       Dongguan Shinein Electronic Technology Co., Ltd. (Shinein) was founded in 2017. It is a semiconductor packaging enterprise specializing in the design, production, and sales of temperature sensors. The company’s R&D team consists of engineers with years of experience in Japanese and Taiwanese companies. The product processes inherit the valuable experience accumulated by foreign enterprises and continuously innovate on traditional practices. After years of market accumulation, the company’s products have been widely used in temperature control for new energy vehicles, photovoltaics, energy storage, and household appliances, and have been highly recognized by many listed companies such as BYD Auto, Dinxin Communication, and Yimikang Technology.
